Concerto (2017) (7')

A concerto for piano and orchestra. Commissioned by the JRNCM Symphony Orchestra.

Instrumentation: piccolo, 2 flutes, 2 oboes, English horn, piccolo clarinet in E-flat, 2 B-flat clarinets, 2 bassoons, 4 horns, piccolo trumpet in D, 2 trumpets, 2 tenor trombones, bass trombone, tuba, 4 timpani+suspended cymbal, 3 percussionists (triangle, suspended cymbal, snare drum, bass drum, tam-tam, glockenspiel, vibraphone, 4.3-octave marimba), celesta, harp, solo piano, strings.

Ringtone Variations (2016) (4')

A set of variations on the well-known Original and Marimba Apple ringtones, for percussion quartet (xylophone+glockenspiel, vibraphone, 5-octave marimba shared between 2 players).

Variations (2015) (5'30")

A set of jazzy variations for piano four hands.

Blue (2012) (6')

A piece for two pianos inspired by the sea. (Written when I was 12.)
